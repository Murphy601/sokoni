# Branding: Names & Logo Concepts

## Naming strategy

A good name for this product should be: short, easy to say on a voice note, easy to type after
`wa.me/`, meaningful in Swahili/East Africa (for local trust) but pronounceable globally (for later
expansion), and not already a registered marketplace trademark.

## Shortlist

| # | Name | Meaning | Why it works | Tagline |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Sokoni** | Swahili: "at the market" | Sounds exotic/ownable internationally, instantly familiar locally, 3 syllables, no clash with major brands | "Your Market, On WhatsApp." |
| 2 | PataMall | "Pata" = get/find | Playful "mall" suffix signals variety/marketplace, "Pata" is an easy CTA verb | "Pata Anything. Instantly." |
| 3 | DukaLink | "Duka" = shop, + "Link" | Literally describes the product — links you to shops | "Every Duka. One Chat Away." |
| 4 | NunuaBot | "Nunua" = buy | Very literal, bot-forward, good for an AI-first brand story | "Just Say What You Want to Buy." |
| 5 | ChapChap Mall | "Chap chap" = quickly | High energy, fun for Reels/TikTok marketing | "Shop Chap Chap — Anywhere." |
| 6 | Sokoni AI | Sokoni + AI | If you want "AI" explicitly in the name for positioning | "The AI Mall in Your Pocket." |

**Recommendation:** go with **Sokoni** as the primary brand (clean, ownable, short), and keep
PataMall and DukaLink as backups in case the domain/trademark/WhatsApp Business name is taken.

Check availability of:

- **Domain:** sokoni.africa, sokoni.shop, getsokoni.com, sokoniapp.com (sokoni.com itself is very
  likely taken)
- **WhatsApp Business display name:** "Sokoni" or "Sokoni AI"
- **Instagram/TikTok handle:** @sokoni.ke or @shopsokoni

## Logo concepts

Three logo directions accompany the name shortlist (see `assets/logos/`):

1. **sokoni-logo.png** — a friendly rounded shopping-basket/chat-bubble hybrid mark in green +
   deep purple (matches WhatsApp green for instant visual association, plus a distinct purple to
   stand apart from WhatsApp itself), wordmark "Sokoni" in a rounded modern sans-serif.
2. **patamall-logo.png** — a bold "mall/storefront" roof shape merged with a location-pin/checkmark,
   orange + navy palette (energetic, marketplace feel), wordmark "PataMall".
3. **dukalink-logo.png** — a chain-link icon merged into a shop-front silhouette, teal + gold
   palette, wordmark "DukaLink".

Use whichever resonates — all three are designed to work as a small circular WhatsApp profile photo
(the icon must be legible at ~192×192px) and as a horizontal lockup with wordmark for the website
header.

## Visual/brand system suggestions

- **Primary color:** WhatsApp-adjacent green (`#25D366`-ish) for the WhatsApp CTA button so it feels
  native, paired with a distinctive brand purple or navy (`#1B1035` / `#0B1A3A`) so the brand itself
  doesn't look like a WhatsApp clone.
- **Type:** a rounded, friendly sans-serif for the wordmark (e.g. Poppins/Baloo 2/Nunito) to feel
  approachable, not corporate.
- **Tone of voice:** warm, fast, a little playful (mirrors Sheng/Swahili slang like "chap chap",
  "poa", "sawa") — this is what makes reference shopping Reels feel relatable.
- **Iconography:** basket, chat bubble, location pin, and a subtle African motif (kitenge pattern
  accents) used sparingly in social content, not in the core logo (keep the core logo clean for
  small sizes).
