Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026 · Applies to sokonimall.com, Sokoni Mall on WhatsApp, supplier applications, and pickup point applications

Sokoni Mall Enterprise (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices when you use our website and WhatsApp shopping assistant.

1. Information we collect

  • WhatsApp messages — text you send to our business number, including order details, names, delivery addresses, and phone numbers.
  • Order data — products ordered, order IDs, status, and payment status (e.g. COD, M-Pesa till).
  • Payment claims — when you reply paid on WhatsApp, we record your payment notice and share order details with admin for M-Pesa till verification.
  • Reviews — star ratings and comments you submit on WhatsApp or our website.
  • Supplier applications — if you apply at sokonimall.com/suppliers, we collect business name, contact person, phone, email, city, delivery capability, product details, and supply prices you submit.
  • Pickup point applications — if you apply at sokonimall.com/pickup-points, we collect shop name, contact person, phone, email, county, town, street address, landmark, opening hours, shop type, M-Pesa number for commissions, storage and security details, and any notes you provide.
  • Website usage — browser type, pages viewed, and preferences stored locally (e.g. dark mode, currency toggle) via localStorage.
  • Technical logs — our servers may log IP addresses, timestamps, and error data for security and debugging.

We do not intentionally collect passwords or payment card numbers through WhatsApp. For local COD orders you pay via M-Pesa Till 4775847 on delivery — not to riders.

2. How we use information

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order updates and respond to support messages
  • Review supplier and pickup point applications, onboard approved partners, and manage commissions or payouts
  • Improve our AI assistant and product recommendations
  • Display customer reviews on our website (we may show your first name and comment)
  • Post marketing content (e.g. TikTok) featuring products — not your private messages
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Conversational AI & data security

By interacting with our WhatsApp AI shopping assistant, you agree to collection and processing of data necessary to fulfill requests:

  • What we collect: WhatsApp phone number, delivery address, and text prompts to find products and route deliveries.
  • How we use it: Fulfill orders, improve search accuracy, send automated status updates, and train our shopping assistant.
  • Data protection: We do not sell or lease your chat history or phone number to third-party marketers.
  • Third-party AI: Secure API layers process search requests. No financial PINs or card data are shared with AI sub-processors.

4. AI processing (technical)

Free-text messages may be processed by third-party AI providers (e.g. via OpenRouter) to generate shopping replies. Do not send sensitive personal data (ID numbers, health information, bank PINs) in chat.

5. Third-party services

We use trusted providers to run Sokoni, including:

  • WhatsApp / WAHA — messaging delivery
  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security
  • AI providers — conversational replies
  • TikTok — social posting (when you connect our business account)
  • Affiliate & partner stores — when you follow external product links

Each third party has its own privacy policy. WhatsApp is operated by Meta; your use of WhatsApp is also subject to Meta’s policies.

6. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Delivery riders, logistics partners, or pickup points (name, phone, and order details needed for delivery or collection)
  • Suppliers — only what is needed to fulfill an order. By default, suppliers receive product and payout details, not your contact. If a supplier is delivering directly to you, Sokoni admin may share your name, phone, and delivery location with that supplier.
  • Pickup points — if you choose to collect from a Sokoni pickup point, we share your first name, phone number, and order ID with that partner so they can verify you and release the parcel. Your full home address is not shared unless required for a specific arrangement.
  • Service providers who help us operate the bot and website
  • Authorities when required by law

Supplier applicants: we use your business contact details to review your application and communicate approval or rejection on WhatsApp. Supply prices you submit are used internally to set retail prices; they are not shown to customers.

Pickup point applicants: we use your shop and location details to assess suitability, display approved pickup points on our website (shop name, area, and opening hours — not your personal phone on the public list unless you agree), and pay commissions to the M-Pesa number you provide. We may contact you on WhatsApp for operational updates.

7. Retention

We keep order and contact records as long as needed for business, legal, and support purposes, then delete or anonymise them when no longer required.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical measures (HTTPS, access controls, admin-only commands). No online service is 100% secure; use WhatsApp and our site at your own risk.

9. Your rights

Under applicable Kenyan data-protection law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us on WhatsApp and we will respond within a reasonable time.

10. Children

Sokoni is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. International transfers

Some providers (AI, Cloudflare, affiliate platforms) may process data outside Kenya. We use them only where necessary to provide the service.

12. Changes

We may update this Policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Material changes may be announced on our website or WhatsApp channel.
