Supplier programme · Kenya countrywide

Sell through Sokoni — we bring the buyers on WhatsApp

You list supply prices. Sokoni sets customer retail, takes orders on WhatsApp, and pays you after delivery when cash or M-Pesa is collected.

How it works

  1. Submit your business + products with your supply price.
  2. Sokoni reviews and publishes retail prices (customers never see your cost).
  3. When someone orders, we WhatsApp you to fulfill — delivery is coordinated in chat.
  4. After the order is delivered, we owe you your supply amount. Payout when marked paid.

Pricing model

Customer retail ≈ your supply price + KES 100 + 8%, rounded to the nearest KES 50. You only submit supply price; Sokoni confirms final shelf price on approval.

Example: supply KES 3,000 → retail about KES 3,350.

🚚

Flexible delivery

Some suppliers deliver to buyers; others drop at our hub or hand to a rider. We decide per order and location — all coordinated on WhatsApp.

🔒

Customer privacy

Buyer phone and address are not shared by default. Sokoni admin only shares contact when you are delivering directly to the customer.

Countrywide
