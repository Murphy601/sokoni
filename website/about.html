About Sokoni Mall

Kenyan-born · Founded by David Thuku Muiruri · sokonimall.com

Sokoni Mall Enterprise (sokonimall.com) is a homegrown conversational commerce platform. We built a smart AI shopping assistant that lives inside WhatsApp — so you can discover products, place orders, and track deliveries without downloading another heavy shopping app.

Our mission

Bridge cutting-edge automated retail with absolute consumer trust in Kenya. We process requests in English, Kiswahili, and Sheng, matching shoppers to local distributor inventory and curated international affiliate deals.
