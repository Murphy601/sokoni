Frequently Asked Questions

Sokoni Mall · Public Beta · sokonimall.com

How does shopping on WhatsApp work?

No app download or account needed. Message our AI assistant on +254 117 422 428 in English, Kiswahili, or Sheng. It searches our local catalog, places your order, and sends tracking updates in the same chat.

Why does the M-Pesa Till show a personal name?

Sokoni Mall is in live Public Beta Testing. While corporate registrations are finalized, authorized local payments go through Buy Goods Till 4775847, legally registered to our founder David Thuku Muiruri as a sole-proprietor bridge account during launch.

Do I need to pay a commitment or delivery fee upfront?

Absolutely not. For all local catalog items we enforce strict Pay on Delivery. Never send money before the item is in your hands and fully inspected.

What are international orders?

Products from overseas marketplaces (AliExpress, Temu, Amazon, etc.) link you to partner stores. Sokoni may earn a small affiliate commission at no extra cost to you. International items do not support pay-on-delivery and are subject to partner checkout, shipping, and Kenya customs rules.

How do I track my package?

Every order gets an ID like SK-1042. Type your order number or track in WhatsApp for a live status timeline.

What offers or discounts are available?

During beta, promotional offers are capped at 3% off eligible local orders using code SOKONI3 in chat. We do not run larger percentage discounts or upfront payment deals on local COD orders.
